Shoe Repair &
Leather Services

1

Deep Clean Shoe

This cleaning process removes built-up dirt and stains. Start by removing laces and insoles, then pre-treat stains. Clean with a soft brush and mild solution, rinse well, and dry completely. Finish by conditioning the material to prevent cracking.
2

Deep Clean Ransel

Deep cleaning a Ransel backpack helps maintain its condition and extend its life. Empty the bag, remove straps, and shake off dirt. Spot-clean stains, then scrub gently with a soft brush and mild cleaner.
3

Hand Stitching Regular

Deep cleaning a Ransel backpack keeps it in top shape. Empty it, remove straps, and shake off dirt. Spot-clean stains, then wash with a gentle solution using a soft brush to remove grime and odors.
4

Repaint Canvas Sneakers

Repainting canvas sneakers revives old shoes. Choose paint made for canvas, remove laces and dirt, and tape off areas you don't want painted. Apply a base coat, let it dry, then add a second coat if needed for even coverage.
